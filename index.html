About the MIOP Team:

The MIOP team is made up of three current college players, those being Robert Savely (OF), Cooper Poggioli (C), and Zachary Rechtshaid (P). All members of the team are engineering majors at Stevens Institute of Technology and are here to improve your approach.

MIOP's Mission:

Our mission is to give the hitter a view into the analytic side of the game by giving them a way to sharpen their approach against every type of pitcher. We guarantee that MIOP will help you gameplan and predicate what the pitcher is going to throw to you based on our algorithm that was meticulously crafted to cover every situation.
